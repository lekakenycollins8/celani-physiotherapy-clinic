# Homepage Structure

# Homepage Goals

The homepage must:

- Build trust immediately
- Explain clinic expertise quickly
- Increase appointment inquiries
- Increase WhatsApp conversations
- Improve SEO authority
- Encourage exploration of services

---

# Homepage Flow

1. Hero Section
2. Trust Indicators
3. Services Preview
4. Why Choose CELANI
5. Recovery Journey
6. Testimonials
7. FAQ
8. CTA Banner
9. Footer

---

# 1. Hero Section

## Objectives

- Establish trust instantly
- Clarify services
- Encourage contact
- Create premium first impression

---

## Layout

Two-column desktop layout.

Left:
- Heading
- Supporting copy
- CTA buttons

Right:
- Premium rehabilitation imagery

---

## Primary CTA

Book Appointment

## Secondary CTA

WhatsApp Consultation

---

## UX Notes

- Large typography
- Strong spacing
- Minimal clutter
- Clear hierarchy
