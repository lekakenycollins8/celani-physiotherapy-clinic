# CELANI Physiotherapy & Sports Injuries Clinic
# Brand Strategy

## Brand Positioning

CELANI Physiotherapy and Sports Injuries Clinic positions itself as a premium physiotherapy and rehabilitation clinic focused on professional, evidence-based recovery care.

The clinic should communicate:

- Trust
- Professionalism
- Modern rehabilitation expertise
- Patient-centered care
- Recovery-focused treatment
- Calm and reassuring medical professionalism

The visual and digital experience should compete with premium international rehabilitation clinics.

---

# Core Brand Promise

Professional physiotherapy care you can trust.

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

- Premium
- Modern
- Clean
- Calm
- Human-centered
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Recovery-focused

Avoid:

- Generic hospital branding
- Overly clinical aesthetics
- Corporate stiffness
- Overly colorful interfaces
- Aggressive medical marketing

---

# Emotional Objectives

Patients should feel:

- Safe
- Heard
- Professionally supported
- Hopeful about recovery
- Confident in treatment quality

---

# Audience Profile

## Primary Audience

Adults in Nairobi seeking:

- Physiotherapy treatment
- Sports rehabilitation
- Orthopedic rehabilitation
- Back and neck pain treatment
- Pelvic floor rehabilitation
- Post-surgery rehabilitation

---

# User Intent Categories

## High Intent

- Book appointment
- Call clinic
- WhatsApp inquiry
- Locate clinic

## Research Intent

- Learn about services
- Understand recovery process
- Compare treatment options
- Evaluate clinic credibility

---

# Competitive Direction

The website should visually align with:

- Premium physiotherapy clinics
- Sports rehabilitation centers
- Modern healthcare startups
- High-end wellness clinics

The website should NOT resemble:

- Public hospital websites
- Outdated medical websites
- Template-heavy local clinic websites
